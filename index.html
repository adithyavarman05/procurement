Administrator Action Required: Manual Verification

To complete vendor verification, please follow these steps after the form is submitted:

  1. Take the Udyam Registration Number provided by the vendor.
  2. Go to the official Government of India MSME site: Udyam Registration Portal.
  3. Click on the "Verify Udyam Registration Number" link.
  4. Enter the vendor's Udyam number and the on-screen CAPTCHA.
  5. Confirm that the business name and details on the portal match the details submitted here.
